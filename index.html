Acts & Rules

Road Transport Corporations Act, 1950

Enacted by the Parliament to establish statutory corporations to provide efficient road transport services in the states.

Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989

Direct laws governing registration, licensing, speed limits, vehicle emissions, passenger safety precautions, and conductor duties.

Department Circulars

Citizen Charter

Passenger Rights & Commuter Guarantees

1. Right to safe, clean, reliable and timely bus transport services.
2. Access to clear schedule charts and fare grids across every depot.
3. Right to prompt grievance resolution within 14 working days of filing a complaint.
